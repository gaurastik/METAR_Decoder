India Meterological Department

The India Meteorological Department (IMD) is the national meteorological service of India, responsible for providing weather forecasting, climate monitoring, and related services. Established in 1875, the IMD plays a crucial role in safeguarding lives and properties by issuing timely weather warnings and advisories. With its vast network of observatories, satellites, and advanced forecasting models, the IMD helps in predicting and understanding weather patterns, cyclones, monsoons, and other atmospheric phenomena. The department's efforts contribute to various sectors such as agriculture, aviation, disaster management, and public safety, supporting the overall development and well-being of the nation.
